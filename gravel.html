Frame

Ostro carbon monocoque

A high-modulus T800 carbon-fibre frame, laid up as a single shell with flat-mount disc tabs and 12×142 thru-axle. Stiff where it drives, compliant where the ground gets rough — one structure, no bonded junctions.

T800 carbon · 8.7 kg net

Build

Shimano GRX, geared for dirt

A 1×11 Shimano GRX600 groupset on Shimano RX400 hydraulic discs — a single, wide-range front ring so there's nothing to drop on a rough descent and one clean line from lever to rotor.

GRX600 1×11 · hydraulic disc

Ride

Rolls over the rough

700c wheels on wide 2.1" tyres and a full carbon fork: grip and float on loose ground, momentum on the flat. Light, planted and unbothered when the tarmac runs out.
